Background of asian cuisine

There are various significant regional cuisines in Asia. Central Asian cuisine, East Asian cuisine, North Asian cuisine, South Asian cuisine, Southeast Asian cuisine, and West Asian cuisine are involved. A cuisine is a distinct range of cooking methods and traditions associated with a particular culture. Asia, being the world’s biggest and most populous continent, is home to a diverse range of cultures, each with its own distinct cuisine. Rice is an example of a staple starch in almost all Asian dietary cultures.
